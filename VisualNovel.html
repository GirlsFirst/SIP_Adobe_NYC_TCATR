The Visual Novel - The Catcher in the Raichu

The Catcher in the Raichu Visual Novel is a product of our group's Final Project, which was completed in August 2016. This Visual Novel, Pokemon ediction, allows the player to enter into the world of Pokemon where they can choose their name, their archnemesis' name, their Pokemon companions and what tasks they wish to achieve at different parts of their adventure.
